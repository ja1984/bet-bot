## Key Patterns & Trends

### What consistently works

2. **Over 2.5 in CL knockouts** — hit in nearly every CL game analysed. Goals happen when top teams clash.
3. **1H Over 0.5 as an anchor** — 75% hit rate. Games with quality attacking teams almost always produce a first-half goal.
4. **"THE GOALS GAME" identification** — Villa-Chelsea (5 goals), PSG-Chelsea (7 goals), Atalanta-Bayern (7 goals). When correctly identified, these deliver massive returns.
5. **Defensive hedges** — Under-based combos have saved multiple nights (March 3, March 11, March 12).

1. **Result markets as high-confidence picks** — City Win 9/10 ❌, RM Win 9/10 ❌, Fulham Win 8/10 ❌, Liverpool Win 7/10 ❌. The 40% hit rate proves the framework's cap at 8/10 is correct.

3. **Trebles with no safe leg** — March 3 C4 went 0/3, March 4 C4 failed. Fun bets need anchoring.
4. **"Must chase" in EL** — teams lack the quality. This was the Feb 26 disaster.
5. **Backing favourites on single match nights without a hedge** — March 2 was 0/4 because every combo needed RM to win.
